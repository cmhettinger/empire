Design notes

- `market` and `ticker` are exact, case-sensitive provider values. Empty values
  and surrounding whitespace are rejected; no normalized shadow columns exist.
- `first_seen` and `last_seen` are the minimum and maximum accepted trading
  dates observed for the series, not listing validity or active-status dates;
  they are either both null or both populated.
- No status, currency, adjustment, adjusted-close, arbitrary metadata,
  source-snapshot, or Core-run columns are stored.
- Core runs, raw objects, and source snapshots remain operational import records
  but are intentionally not linked from individual provider listings or bars.
- `change` is close minus the previous stored bar's close; `changepct` is that
  change divided by the previous close. Both are null for the first stored bar,
  and `changepct` is null when the previous close is zero.
- `typ`, `hl_range`, and `oc_range` persist `(high + low + close) / 3`,
  `high - low`, and `close - open`. All derived values use scale 8.
- Historical inserts and corrections recalculate prior-close-derived values on
  the immediately following stored bar.
